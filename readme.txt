A magazine structure of posts and taxonomies.

== Installation ==

1. Unzip the archive and put the `eu-cookies` folder into your plugins folder (`/wp-content/plugins/`).
1. Ensure John's ExtendedCPTs and ExtendedTaxos libraries are loaded
1. Install and activate the [Posts 2 Posts plugin](http://wordpress.org/plugins/posts-to-posts/)
1. Activate the plugin from the Plugins menu.
